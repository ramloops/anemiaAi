Transforming Anemia Detection

Revolutionary AI technology making anemia screening accessible worldwide

The Current Problem

  • Anemia affects nearly 1 in 4 people globally, representing a major public health concern
  • Blood tests remain the only available diagnostic method for anemia detection
  • Laboratory testing is expensive and often inaccessible in underserved regions
  • Point-of-care testing accuracy decreases to 70% under field conditions
  • Healthcare systems face significant costs from daily phlebotomy procedures

Our AI-Powered Solution

  • Smartphone camera technology enables detection using standard mobile devices
  • Advanced AI algorithms analyze eye images for accurate anemia screening
  • Completely noninvasive approach eliminates need for blood draws
  • Cost-effective solution accessible in any location with smartphone connectivity
  • Longitudinal monitoring capabilities support ongoing patient care

Telemedicine Applications

  • Seamless integration with existing patient portals and healthcare gateways
  • Standard screening component for annual physical examinations
  • Regular monitoring for patients receiving anticoagulation therapy
  • Continuous assessment support for oncology patients during treatment
  • Monitoring capabilities for patients with chronic kidney disease

Global Health Impact

  • Serving populations in underserved areas without laboratory facilities
  • Supporting the 40% of pregnant women at risk for anemia worldwide
  • Enabling pediatric screening programs in developing regions
  • Reducing healthcare disparities through accessible diagnostic technology
  • Facilitating cost-effective population-level health screening initiatives

About AnemiaAI

Pioneering the future of anemia detection through innovative artificial intelligence

Our Mission

To eliminate barriers to anemia detection by developing accessible, accurate, and affordable screening technology. We are dedicated to improving global health outcomes through groundbreaking AI solutions that can reach underserved populations and transform healthcare delivery worldwide.

Advanced Technology

Our proprietary artificial intelligence model processes eye images captured by standard smartphone cameras to detect and monitor anemia with clinical accuracy. This innovative approach maintains diagnostic precision while completely eliminating the need for invasive blood draws.

Clinical Excellence

Through partnerships with Mass General Brigham and participation in the MESH Incubator program, we ensure our technology meets the highest standards of clinical validation, regulatory compliance, and medical excellence required for healthcare innovation.

Global Impact Vision

Our technology has the potential to transform healthcare access for millions worldwide, from pregnant women in resource-constrained environments to cancer patients requiring regular monitoring, creating a more equitable healthcare landscape.
